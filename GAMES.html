FPS GAMES

VALORANT

The game that I've spent a lot of time with for the past 2 years very enjoyable game, but it can be stressful, especially if you play competitively and are concerned about your rank. I was once a decent player, playing very well in every game, participating in competition tournaments, and other stuff; I was once Immortal 3 rank #2556 in Asia, but now I'm stuck as descendant 2, haven't played much due to school and studies, but all were very fun experiences, especially when you played with friends and had fun. I'll rate Valorant an 8/10.

BATTLEFIELD 1

One of the best FPS games I've played I really enjoy playing every Battlefield game (BF1, BF2, BF3, BF5) since the game focuses on war, and since I myself am very interested in history, I really wanted to know and learn more by playing war games because you can really enjoy the experience because of the graphics, the sound effects, ect. It's a very nice game to play with friends, and you can get to know a lot of people. I'll give battlefield games in general a solid 10/10.

OVERWATCH 2

I recently downloaded this game and it's a lot of fun; I really enjoy playing it. I don't have much to say about the game other than it's enjoyable; I'll give it a 6.5/10 for the time being.

ADVENTURE / PUZZLE GAMES

GENSHIN IMPACT

Also one of the best games I've played; it has very fun and relaxing game stories, a wonderful community, and I haven't met a single toxic person in my two years of playing multiplayer; it's truly a stress-free game. I'll give it a solid 9.5/10.

SEKIRO

The gameplay is focused on stealth, exploration, and combat, with a particular emphasis on boss battles. The game takes place in a fictionalized Japan during the Sengoku period and makes strong references to Buddhist mythology and philosophy. While making the game, lead director Hidetaka Miyazaki wanted to create a new intellectual property (IP) that marked a departure from the Dark Souls series of games, also made by FromSoftware. I'll give it a 7/10.

LITTLE NIGHTMARES II

Little Nightmares II is a puzzle-platform horror adventure game developed by Tarsier Studios, published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It is the prequel to 2017's Little Nightmares. I'll give it a 8.5/10

SANDBOX GAMES

TERRIA

Sanbox games are games that I grew up with, and one of them is Terria. One of the best games I've ever played, especially with friends; while the graphics aren't great because it's 8-bit, the gameplay and mechanics are fantastic. I'll give it a solid 9.

ROBLOX

Roblox was the most memorable game I played from 2015 to 2019. I spent my entire gaming life playing Roblox, and the game that I spent a lot of money on is just amazing; I made a lot of memories, made a lot of friends, and enjoyed the game. 

MINECRAFT

Minecraft is still the best game I've ever played, with over 1,000 hours spent with friends on survival, building houses, finding diamonds, getting good armor, and finishing the game. It's just sad that after thousands of hours of playing on that server or in that world, people you play with stop playing, and it's starting to die; we just leave all the good memories. Minecraft BEST GAME 11/10.
